# Privacy Policy for Recaller

**Last updated: 22/May/2026**

Recaller (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

## 1. Information We Collect
Recaller does **not collect personal data on external servers**.

All data you enter (such as names, notes, dates, memories, reminders) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## 2. How We Use Information
Your data is used only to:
- display your content
- manage reminders
- provide app functionality

## 3. Data Sharing
We do **not**:
- sell data
- share data
- upload data to servers

## 4. Notifications
The app may send **local notifications** (reminders). These are generated on your device only.

## 5. Data Security
All app data is stored locally on your device. Recaller does not provide cloud backup or account-based recovery. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data may permanently delete your saved information.

### 5.1 Permissions
Recaller may request notification permission so it can send reminders. If you add photos to memories, the app may request access to photos/media only so you can choose images for your own saved memories.

### 5.2 Third-party services
Recaller does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or cloud storage services.

### 5.3 User control
You can delete people, notes, plans, memories, photos, and other saved information inside the app. You can also delete all local app data by uninstalling the app.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
Recaller is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Changes
This policy may be updated.
