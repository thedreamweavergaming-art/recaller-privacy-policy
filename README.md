# Privacy Policy for Recaller

**Last updated: 04/May/2026**

Recaller (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

## 1. Information We Collect
Recaller does **not collect personal data on external servers**.

All data you enter (such as names, notes, dates, memories, reminders) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## 2. How We Use Information
Your data is used only to:
- display your content
- manage reminders
- provide app functionality

## 3. Data Sharing
We do **not**:
- sell data
- share data
- upload data to servers

## 4. Notifications
The app may send **local notifications** (reminders). These are generated on your device only.

## 5. Data Security
All data is stored locally. **Uninstalling** the app **will delete** your data.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 7. Changes
This policy may be updated.
